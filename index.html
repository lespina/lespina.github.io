About Me

Coder.   Physics Nerd.   Dancer.   Techno & Disco enthusiast.

into vast, complex systems. Despite wonderful research opportunities with NASA and the National

them. Since then, I have taken roles in software at Evernote & Meta, where I have enjoyed
a wealth of opportunities to lead various projects in Growth, Notifications, User Reporting & other areas.
